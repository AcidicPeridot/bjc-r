We would now like a list of us and all our descendants in no particular order (i.e., our children, their children, etc.) by writing the block . This problem is harder than because we can have many children, but it is simpler because we only need a single helper block which reports a list of all our children (empty if we have no kids!). Examples:

  • children(j) reports (k l m)
  • children(p) reports (s)
  • children(l) reports ()
  • me-and-my-descendants(j) reports (j k l m n o p q r s)
  • me-and-my-descendants(k) reports (k n o)
  • me-and-my-descendants(l) reports (l)
    •

You can find a framework for the block under the "Descendants" sprite in the starter file. Do not use any iteration (loops) to complete this block - you should only use recursion and/or HOFs.
