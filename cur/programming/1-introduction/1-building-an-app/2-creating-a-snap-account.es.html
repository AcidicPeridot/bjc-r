Guardar proyectos Snap!

En esta página, aprenderás a usar la aplicación Snap!

  1. There are different ways to save Snap! projects. Some of them involve logging in or creating a Snap! account. Your teacher will let you know which option to use.

    Option A: Save your project to your computer

    You can save this file to your computer, a USB drive, Google Drive, or any other place you keep files.

    In Snap!, go to the File menu (), choose "Save," and select “Computer” from the save dialog:

    The project will be saved in the browser’s download location as an XML file. If you are saving the Click Alonzo game from the sample project, the default filename is "U1L1-ClickAlonzo."

    To open an XML file, drag the file and drop it into Snap! or use "Open..." from the File menu (). This is always an option, even if you have a Snap! account.

    Option B: Your teacher has set up an account for you

    Your teacher will let you know your Snap! username and password. If you ever need to reset your password, the request will go to your teacher.

    Para utilizar correctamente Snap! debes iniciar una sesión.

    Haz clic en el menú Nube () en la barra de herramientas. (If you only see the outline of a cloud, that means no one is logged in.)

    • Si aparece la palabra "Cerrar sesión" junto a tu nombre de usuario, entonces estás dentro.
    • Si aparece el nombre de otra persona, entonces presiona "Cerrar sesión" e ingresa con tu cuenta.
    • En otro caso, selecciona la opción "Iniciar sesión..." e ingresa tu usuario y tu contraseña para comenzar.

    Podrás cambiar tu contraseña en cualquier momento desde el menú Nube, .

    To save your project:

    • Go to the File menu () and choose "Save As..."
    • If you are saving the Click Alonzo game from the sample project, the default filename is "U1L1-ClickAlonzo."
    • Click on "Save."

    Recuerda cerrar sesión en Snap! (utilizando el menú Nube) al terminar tu trabajoen una computadora pública.

    Option C: Create a Snap! account with a teacher email

    This is a Snap! account where any password reset requests will go to your teacher's email account.

    Open a new tab or window and go to http://snap.berkeley.edu/sign_up.

    • Make sure your username does not include any personal information (no names, birthdays, student ID numbers, etc.) Same goes for your password.
    • You will use your teacher's email, so you can enter in any birthmonth and choose the last option (before a certain year) for the year.
    • In the "email address" box, enter the email address your teacher gives you.

    After you create the account, go back to your Snap! window and log in using your new username and password. Click the Cloud menu and select "Login..."

    Podrás cambiar tu contraseña en cualquier momento desde el menú Nube, .

    Option D: Create a Snap! account with your personal email

    This is a Snap! account where any password reset requests will go to your personal email account.

    Open a new tab or window and go to http://snap.berkeley.edu/sign_up.

    • Make sure your username does not include any personal information (no names, birthdays, student ID numbers, etc.) Same goes for your password.
    • You must be 13 or older to open a Snap! account. Enter in your birth month and year.
    • In the "email address" box, enter your personal email. Tendrás que verificar tu cuenta haciendo clic en un enlace que recibirás en tu correo electrónico. Deberías hacerlo en los próximos 3 días. No es necesario que lo hagas de inmediato.

    After you create the account, go back to your Snap! window and log in using your new username and password. Click the Cloud menu () and select "Login..."
